About Whether?

Have you ever gone to class in a jacket and ended up sweating? Or left home in a t-shirt and froze later on? We've all been there.

Whether is a fun and helpful app made just for college students. It gives you real-time weather updates, smart outfit suggestions, and even lets you hear what other students are saying through a live forum.

Whether you're on your way to class, the gym, or a campus event — Whether helps you dress right and feel confident no matter what the weather decides to do.
