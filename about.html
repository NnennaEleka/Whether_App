About Whether?

Have you ever gone to class with a jacket and ended up sweating? Or left home in a t-shirt and froze later?

Welcome to Whether? – an app designed to help college students dress smarter by giving real-time weather info, outfit suggestions, and live campus feedback from fellow students.
